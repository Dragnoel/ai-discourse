# Lanfang Sun

Lanfang Sun is a co-author of Jin et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study uses metaphor analysis to examine 277 international postgraduate students' perceptions of GenAI in academic writing at UK universities.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Jin et al. (2025) validate their metaphor-elicitation survey by collecting screenshots of participants' actual GenAI interactions in a second data collection phase, enabling cross-checking of stated metaphors against observed use patterns — a methodological contribution to research on AI perception.
