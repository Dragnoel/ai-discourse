# Margaret Bearman

Margaret Bearman is an education researcher at the Centre for Research in Assessment and Digital Learning (CRADLE), Deakin University, Melbourne, Australia. In this collection she is the lead author of Bearman et al. (2023), a critical discourse analysis of how AI is constructed in higher education research literature.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Bearman et al. (2023) is the source of the [[Discourse of Imperative Change]] and [[Discourse of Altering Authority]] concepts, which characterise the two dominant ways AI is framed in the peer-reviewed HE literature. The paper's central argument — that the HE AI literature overwhelmingly neglects social and ethical implications in favour of technical framings — has relevance across several themes in this wiki.
