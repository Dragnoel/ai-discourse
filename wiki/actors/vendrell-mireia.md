# Mireia Vendrell

Mireia Vendrell is a researcher at IIIA-CSIC (Artificial Intelligence Research Institute, Spanish National Research Council), Barcelona. In this collection she is co-author of Vendrell and Johnston (2026), a conceptual and normative pedagogical framework for integrating LLMs in higher education in ways that preserve and extend critical thinking.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Vendrell and Johnston (2026) identify three risks of unstructured LLM use — cognitive offloading, metacognitive disengagement, and epistemic narrowing — and develop eight actionable design principles to mitigate them. The framework is explicitly normative: it synthesises evidence to prescribe conditions for pedagogically sound LLM use rather than recommending wholesale exclusion or uncritical adoption.
