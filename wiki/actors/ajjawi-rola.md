# Rola Ajjawi

Rola Ajjawi is a researcher at Deakin University, Australia. In this collection she is a co-author of Bearman et al. (2023), a critical literature review examining how AI is discursively constructed in higher education research journals.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Bearman, Ryan, and Ajjawi (2023) identify a critical gap in the HE AI literature: only 3 of 146 surveyed papers consider ethical implications. The study calls for new research that traces accountability in AI-mediated practices rather than treating AI as a neutral technological innovation.
