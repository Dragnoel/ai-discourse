# Yizhou Fan

Yizhou Fan is a co-author of Cheng et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study uses quantitative ethnographic methods and epistemic network analysis to examine how question-asking in GenAI interactions affects writing performance.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Cheng et al. (2025) find that GenAI participants tended to ask one-off direct questions while human-tutor participants engaged in longer conversational exchanges with more social coordination, suggesting that the interaction structure of GenAI consultations differs qualitatively from that of human tutoring in ways that affect learning processes.
