# Wing Kin Cheng

Wing Kin Cheng is a co-author of Xia et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study validates the GAICS-TR scale for measuring GenAI competency self-efficacy in higher education academics across nine universities.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Xia et al. (2025) address the gap between existing K-12-focused AI competency frameworks and the unique demands of higher education academics, who must integrate GenAI into both teaching and research activities simultaneously — a dual-role context not captured by prior instruments.
