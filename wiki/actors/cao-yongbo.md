# Yongbo Cao

Yongbo Cao is a researcher at Wuhan Huaxia Institute of Technology, China. In this collection he is a co-author of Wang et al. (2026), a study developing a LLM-based pedagogical framework for L2 writing instruction.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Wang, Yin, and Cao (2026) argue that prior GenAI integration in L2 writing has been fragmentary, grounded in single pedagogical approaches rather than a coherent multi-principle framework. Their proposed synthesis of active, inquiry-based, and adaptive learning within a tripartite teacher-AI-learner dynamic addresses this gap.
