# Jin Yuxian

Jin Yuxian is a researcher at Sungkyunkwan University, Seoul, South Korea. In this collection he is the sole author of Yuxian (2025), a 2×2 factorial experiment examining the combined effects of LLM chatbot access and critical thinking guidance on declarative and procedural knowledge acquisition.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Yuxian (2025) finds a synergistic interaction: neither LLM access alone nor critical thinking guidance alone achieves gains across both knowledge types, but combining both does — the LLM mitigates the cognitive load burden of critical thinking guidance while guidance ensures the productive struggle required for procedural learning. The finding provides experimental support for integrating AI tools with metacognitive scaffolding rather than deploying them alone.
