# Rebekkah Middleton

Rebekkah Middleton is a co-author of Nikolic et al. (2024); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study is a systematic literature review of teaching academics' attitudes toward AI and GenAI in higher education.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Nikolic et al. (2024) underscore the necessity for institutional support to navigate GenAI integration in tertiary education, identifying a consistent gap between the generally positive faculty attitudes found in the literature and the limited adoption behaviors that follow without sufficient facilitating conditions.
