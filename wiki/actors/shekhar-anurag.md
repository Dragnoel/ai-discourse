# Anurag Shekhar

Anurag Shekhar is a researcher at the University of Johannesburg, South Africa. In this collection he is lead author of Shekhar and Saurombe (2026), a mixed-methods study of 1,454 Reddit narratives about AI-driven job displacement introducing and operationalising "algorithmic anxiety."

## Key contributions to the discourse

Shekhar and Saurombe (2026) introduce algorithmic anxiety as a workplace syndrome distinct from general technostress, framing it as a violation of the psychological contract between workers and employers. Their thematic analysis identifies seven recurring themes including shattered trust, eroded identity, and devalued expertise, arguing that addressing algorithmic anxiety requires transparent communication and employee involvement in AI governance rather than standard technostress interventions.
