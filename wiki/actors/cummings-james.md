# James Cummings

James Cummings is a co-author of Huang et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study investigates undergraduate students' GenAI-assisted cheating behavior through a moral extension of the Theory of Planned Behavior.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Huang et al. (2025) find that students perceive GenAI integration into education as inevitable, and that class-level policies enforced by instructors exert greater influence on cheating behavior than broader institutional policies — a finding with direct practical implications for course design in GenAI-integrated curricula.
