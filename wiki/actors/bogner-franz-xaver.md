# Franz Xaver Bogner

Franz Xaver Bogner is a researcher at the University of Bayreuth, Germany. In this collection he is the senior author of Conradty et al. (2026), a mixed-methods study of European students' conceptions of AI in school contexts, conducted under the DISCOVERY SPACE Erasmus+ project.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Conradty et al. (2026) apply a dual coding framework — distinguishing functional expectations (System A) from pedagogical values (System B) — to 841 open-ended student responses, finding that most students maintain a clear normative boundary between AI that scaffolds thinking and AI that replaces it, irrespective of their specific role repertoire.
