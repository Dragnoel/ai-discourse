# Guanliang Chen

Guanliang Chen is a co-author of Cheng et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study employs epistemic network analysis to examine help-seeking behavior and writing performance in GenAI versus human-tutor conditions.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Cheng et al. (2025) apply epistemic network analysis to demonstrate that the pattern of question-asking — operationalised as a network of co-occurring discourse moves — mediates the experimental condition's effect on writing performance, providing a methodology for studying AI interaction quality beyond simple usage metrics.
