# Juliana Ryan

Juliana Ryan is a researcher affiliated with La Trobe University, Australia. In this collection she is a co-author of Bearman et al. (2023), a critical literature review of AI discourse in higher education journals.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Bearman, Ryan, and Ajjawi (2023) apply Gee's discourse analytic framework to 29 peer-reviewed articles in leading higher education journals, identifying two dominant Discourses — the Discourse of Imperative Change and the Discourse of Altering Authority — through which AI is constructed in the HE research literature.
