# Banafsheh Karamifar

Banafsheh Karamifar is a researcher at Laurentian University, Canada. In this collection she is co-editor (with Andrea Valente) of Karamifar and Valente (2026), the first volume of an edited series examining AI through cross-cultural discourse analysis and media perspectives, published by Palgrave Macmillan.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Karamifar and Valente (2026) establish a dedicated scholarly venue within the *Postdisciplinary Studies in Discourse* series for cross-cultural analysis of how AI is represented, narrated, and contested across different cultural and media contexts. The volume's framing premise — that AI's meanings are culturally constructed through media discourse and vary systematically across national and linguistic environments — positions it as a primary resource for comparative AI discourse research.
