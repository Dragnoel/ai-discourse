# Ying Xiong

Ying Xiong is a researcher at Wuhan University of Technology, China. In this collection she is co-author of Xu and Xiong (2026), a structural equation modelling study of GenAI acceptance determinants in Chinese foreign language education.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Xu and Xiong (2026) demonstrate that performance expectancy and social influence are the dominant predictors of behavioral intention to use GenAI, replicating core UTAUT patterns, while emotional responses, AI literacy, and AI self-efficacy independently influence actual usage beyond intention. This finding distinguishes intention formation from enacted behavior in technology adoption models for GenAI contexts.
