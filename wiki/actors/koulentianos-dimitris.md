# Dimitris Koulentianos

Dimitris Koulentianos is a co-author of Conradty et al. (2026); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study examines students' conceptions of AI in science classrooms across multiple European countries.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Conradty et al. (2026) identify three recurrent AI role repertoires in student expectations — Tutor, Coach, and Companion orientations — and show that while disciplinary differences are present, the normative boundary between scaffolding and delegation is consistent across profiles.
