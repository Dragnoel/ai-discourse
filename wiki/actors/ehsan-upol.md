# Upol Ehsan

Upol Ehsan is a researcher at the Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta. He works on Human-Centered Explainable AI (HCXAI) and is co-author of Ehsan and Riedl (2024), an opinion article in *Patterns* arguing that a single monolithic definition of explainable AI (XAI) is neither feasible nor desirable.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Ehsan and Riedl (2024) apply the Social Construction of Technology (SCOT) framework to the definitional plurality of XAI, arguing that different stakeholder communities (NLP and computer vision) represent legitimate "relevant social groups" with distinct interpretive flexibilities. Prematurely standardising to one interpretation would close off productive innovation before the technology has matured.
