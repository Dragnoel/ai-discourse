# Ramona Beinstingel

Ramona Beinstingel is a co-author of Bassner et al. (2026); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study is a randomised controlled trial on AI-supported programming education at TU Munich.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Bassner et al. (2026) contribute a performance-learning dissociation framework to the EdTech literature, empirically distinguishing task completion metrics from genuine knowledge acquisition and arguing that these must be tracked separately in AI-integrated educational settings.
