# Nicole Hash

Nicole Hash is a co-author of Huang et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material (the study is associated with Boston University and Central Michigan University). The study is a mixed-methods investigation of academic cheating with GenAI using a modified Theory of Planned Behavior.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Huang et al. (2025) find through focus groups and polling that students show considerable ambivalence about subtle forms of AI-assisted academic work, while clearly disapproving of overt substitution — a gradient that has significant implications for how academic integrity policies should be designed and communicated.
