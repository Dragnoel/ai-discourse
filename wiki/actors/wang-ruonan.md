# Ruonan Wang

Ruonan Wang is a researcher whose institutional affiliation is not specified in this collection's source material. In this collection she is lead author of Wang et al. (2026), which proposes a pedagogical framework integrating LLMs into L2 writing instruction through active, inquiry-based, and adaptive learning principles.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Wang et al. (2026) validate a six-step LLM-based writing instruction process — perceive, understand, transfer, analyse, synthesise, output — showing significant improvements in writing outcomes and motivation in a quasi-experimental study. The study contributes a coherent pedagogical rationale arguing that ChatGPT's benefits are contingent on instructional structure.
