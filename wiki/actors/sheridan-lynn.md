# Lynn Sheridan

Lynn Sheridan is a co-author of Nikolic et al. (2024); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study is a systematic literature review of teaching academics' AI adoption attitudes using the UTAUT framework.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Nikolic et al. (2024) synthesise findings across global contexts to show that faculty adoption behaviors lag behind attitudes — positive in principle but constrained in practice — and that this gap is attributable primarily to inadequate facilitating conditions (training, policy, support) rather than principled resistance.
