# Sofoklis Sotiriou

Sofoklis Sotiriou is a co-author of Conradty et al. (2026), affiliated with the DISCOVERY SPACE Erasmus+ project on science education; specific institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The project supports digital and AI literacy in European secondary classrooms.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Conradty et al. (2026) demonstrate that the educational relevance of AI depends less on technical functionality alone than on how AI affordances are pedagogically framed and aligned with learner agency — a finding derived from one of the largest open-ended student response datasets on AI in European school contexts.
