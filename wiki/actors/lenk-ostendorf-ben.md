# Ben Lenk-Ostendorf

Ben Lenk-Ostendorf is a co-author of Bassner et al. (2026); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study is a three-arm RCT on AI-supported programming education at TU Munich.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Bassner et al. (2026) provide experimental evidence that unrestricted AI assistance created a "comfort trap" in which students' performance preferences misaligned with pedagogical effectiveness, informing debates about the design of AI tools for programming education.
