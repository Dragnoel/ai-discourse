# Musawenkosi Saurombe

Musawenkosi Saurombe is a researcher at the University of Johannesburg, South Africa. In this collection he is co-author of Shekhar and Saurombe (2026), which uses sentiment analysis and thematic coding of Reddit narratives to study algorithmic anxiety in workers facing AI-driven job displacement.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Shekhar and Saurombe (2026) find that surface-level VADER sentiment analysis and deeper BERT-based semantic analysis yield divergent readings of the same Reddit data — a methodological finding with implications for automated AI discourse analysis. Their application of psychological contract theory to algorithmic management extends the construct to technology-mediated labour contexts.
