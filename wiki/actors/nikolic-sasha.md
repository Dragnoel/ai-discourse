# Sasha Nikolic

Sasha Nikolic is a researcher at the University of Wollongong, Australia. In this collection he is the lead author of Nikolic et al. (2024), a systematic literature review of teaching academics' attitudes, intentions, and behaviors toward AI and GenAI in higher education.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Nikolic et al. (2024) synthesise the global faculty AI adoption literature through the UTAUT framework, finding that positive attitudes toward AI's teaching efficiency coexist with significant concerns about academic integrity, accuracy, and the need for training. The review identifies inadequate institutional support as the primary cause of the gap between positive faculty attitudes and actual adoption, rather than principled opposition.
