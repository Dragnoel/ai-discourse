# Ekaterina Jussupow

Ekaterina Jussupow is a researcher in the Department of Business, Law and Economics at the Technical University of Darmstadt (TU Darmstadt), Germany. In this collection she is the lead author of Jussupow et al. (2024), a research note in *MIS Quarterly* providing an integrative conceptual framework for algorithm aversion and appreciation.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Jussupow et al. (2024) is the most recent and most theoretically comprehensive treatment of algorithm aversion in this collection. It reconceptualises algorithm aversion as the preference for humans over algorithms in decision-making, proposes a classification scheme across four decision configurations and two perspectives on algorithms, and argues that the empirical fragmentation in this literature is conceptually tractable. It engages directly with Logg et al. (2019) and Dietvorst et al. (2015) and positions their findings within a larger systematic framework.
