# Simon Moss

Simon Moss is a co-author of Nikolic et al. (2024); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study systematically reviews global literature on teaching academics' AI and GenAI attitudes using the UTAUT framework.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Nikolic et al. (2024) draw on UTAUT's four constructs — performance expectancy, effort expectancy, social influence, and facilitating conditions — to organize a diverse body of empirical findings, providing conceptual coherence across studies that had previously used varying frameworks.
