# Xiaojing Weng

Xiaojing Weng is a co-author of Xia et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study validates the GAICS-TR scale through confirmatory factor analysis with 787 academics from nine universities.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Xia et al. (2025) demonstrate via group comparison analyses that the GAICS-TR scale produces consistent results across gender and age groups, supporting the instrument's generalisability as a measurement tool for higher education GenAI competency research.
