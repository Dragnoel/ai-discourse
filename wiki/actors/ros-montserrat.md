# Montserrat Ros

Montserrat Ros is a co-author of Nikolic et al. (2024); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study examines teaching academics' attitudes, intentions, and behaviors toward AI and GenAI in higher education through a systematic literature review.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Nikolic et al. (2024) draw on global literature to identify skill development implications for students as among the most frequently cited faculty concerns — a finding that connects faculty hesitancy about AI to pedagogical values rather than mere technology resistance.
