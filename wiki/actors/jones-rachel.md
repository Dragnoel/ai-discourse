# Rachel Jones

Rachel Jones is a co-author of Nikolic et al. (2024); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study is a systematic literature review of teaching academics' AI and GenAI attitudes in higher education.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Nikolic et al. (2024) conclude that the necessity for institutional support is the most consistent finding across the global faculty adoption literature, underscoring that individual attitude change alone is insufficient to drive meaningful AI integration without organisational infrastructure.
