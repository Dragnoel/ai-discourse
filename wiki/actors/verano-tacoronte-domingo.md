# Domingo Verano-Tacoronte

Domingo Verano-Tacoronte is a researcher at a Spanish public university (specific institution not stated in this collection's source material). In this collection he is lead author of Verano-Tacoronte et al. (2025), a PLS-SEM study of 249 faculty members examining how three distinct types of technology-related anxiety shape intention to adopt ChatGPT.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Verano-Tacoronte et al. (2025) distinguish three faculty anxiety types — anxiety about the academic profession's future, about personal misuse of ChatGPT, and about negative impacts on student learning — and find that only the latter two significantly inhibit adoption intent. Anxiety about student learning exerts a direct negative effect on behavioral intention independent of perceived effort or performance expectancy.
