# Elisabeth Duursma

Elisabeth Duursma is a co-author of Nikolic et al. (2024); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The systematic review covers teaching academics' AI attitudes, intentions, and behaviors in higher education globally.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Nikolic et al. (2024) find that despite rapid GenAI advancement, research on faculty — as distinct from student — adoption has lagged behind, making it difficult for institutions to develop evidence-based support frameworks. The review addresses this gap by synthesising the available empirical literature through a common theoretical lens.
