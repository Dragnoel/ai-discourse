# Qi Xia

Qi Xia is the lead researcher of Xia et al. (2025); specific institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material, though the study reports participation from nine universities. In this collection she is lead author of the GAICS-TR scale development and validation study in the *Journal of Computers in Education* (Springer, 2025).

## Key contributions to the discourse

Xia et al. (2025) developed and validated the GAICS-TR, the first scale specifically designed to measure Generative AI Competency Self-Efficacy for Teachers and Researchers in higher education. The four-domain, nine-dimension framework addresses a gap in the field: prior AI competency frameworks focused on K-12 education and did not account for the dual teaching-and-research responsibilities of higher education academics.
