# Erin Young

Erin Young is a researcher at the University of Oxford (Oxford Internet Institute), UK. In this collection she is co-author of Eynon and Young (2021), a SCOT-informed interview study examining how academia, industry, and policy stakeholders construct AI for lifelong learning.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Eynon and Young (2021) identify three incompatible technological frames — academic, commercial, and policy — through which AI for lifelong learning is understood, arguing that commercial frames wield disproportionate design influence. The paper is one of three in this collection applying the Social Construction of Technology framework to AI discourse.
