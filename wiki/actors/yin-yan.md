# Yan Yin

Yan Yin is a researcher at Hubei University, China. In this collection she is a co-author of Wang et al. (2026), a study proposing and validating a LLM-based pedagogical framework for active, inquiry-based, and adaptive learning in second language writing.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Wang, Yin, and Cao (2026) demonstrate through a quasi-experimental study with 50 sophomores that their six-step LLM-integrated writing framework significantly enhances writing outcomes and motivation, contributing evidence that structured pedagogical design can unlock ChatGPT's learning benefits in L2 writing contexts.
