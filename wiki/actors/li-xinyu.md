# Xinyu Li

Xinyu Li is a co-author of Cheng et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study investigates how question-asking to GenAI mediates writing performance in a comparative experimental design.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Cheng et al. (2025) contribute an operationalised distinction between pragmatic/direct questions (driven by knowledge deficits) and socially coordinated questions (driven by relationship management), showing that the former type is what mediates performance gains in GenAI conditions.
