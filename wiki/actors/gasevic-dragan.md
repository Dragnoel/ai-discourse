# Dragan Gašević

Dragan Gašević is a researcher at Monash University, Australia, known for work in learning analytics and educational data mining. In this collection he is a co-author of Cheng et al. (2025), a study examining how question-asking patterns in GenAI interactions mediate academic writing performance.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Cheng et al. (2025) employ quantitative ethnographic methods and epistemic network analysis — methods associated with Gašević's research programme in learning analytics — to show that pragmatic, direct knowledge-seeking questions in GenAI interactions mediate performance improvement. The study contributes a mechanism-level account of how GenAI improves educational outcomes.
