# Fangzhou Jin

Fangzhou Jin is a researcher at the University of Hong Kong. In this collection he is the lead author of Jin et al. (2025), a metaphor analysis study of 277 international postgraduate students' perceptions of GenAI in academic writing.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Jin et al. (2025) develop the 4T Pyramid Model, classifying 53 student-generated metaphors for GenAI into four categories — Technical Support, Text Development, Transformative Potential, and Threat — and find that disciplinary background significantly shapes metaphor choice. Most students perceived GenAI as primarily Transformative rather than merely a writing tool, with students in social sciences and humanities more likely to invoke threat metaphors.
