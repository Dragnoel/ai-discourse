# Tianyuan Xu

Tianyuan Xu is a researcher at Zhongnan University of Economics and Law, Wuhan, China. In this collection he is lead author of Xu and Xiong (2026), an SEM study of 409 Chinese foreign language students examining multidimensional determinants of GenAI acceptance using an extended UTAUT model.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Xu and Xiong (2026) expand UTAUT by adding emotions, AI literacy, and AI self-efficacy as constructs, finding that actual GenAI usage is directly influenced by all three alongside behavioral intention. The study validates a cross-national note: Chinese FL students show moderating effects of gender and regional university tier on specific adoption pathways.
