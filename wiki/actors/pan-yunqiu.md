# Yunqiu Pan

Yunqiu Pan is a co-author of Jin et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study employs the 4T Pyramid Model — Technical Support, Text Development, Transformative Potential, Threat — to categorise students' GenAI metaphors in academic writing.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Jin et al. (2025) find that the majority of students across 14 countries and 18 UK universities conceptualised GenAI as primarily Transformative (more than just a writing tool), with the Threat metaphor cluster (dependency, loss of voice, ethical risk) present across all disciplines rather than restricted to humanities students alone.
