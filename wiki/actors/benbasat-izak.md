# Izak Benbasat

Izak Benbasat is a researcher at the University of British Columbia, Canada, known for foundational work in information systems and technology acceptance. In this collection he is a co-author of Jussupow et al. (2024), a conceptual research note in *MIS Quarterly* providing an integrative framework for algorithm aversion and appreciation research.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Jussupow, Benbasat, and Heinzl (2024) diagnose fragmentation in algorithm aversion research as arising from inconsistent definitions and incompatible measurement approaches, and propose a classification scheme based on two dimensions — how algorithms are conceptualised and which decision configurations are used — to enable cumulative theory-building.
