# Yixin Cheng

Yixin Cheng is a researcher at Monash University, Australia. In this collection she is the lead author of Cheng et al. (2025), the first empirical study examining how seeking help from GenAI via question-asking affects academic writing performance.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Cheng et al. (2025) find that GenAI participants asked significantly more questions than human-tutor participants, and that asking direct knowledge-seeking questions mediated the GenAI condition's performance advantage. The study argues that GenAI lowers the social cost of admitting knowledge gaps, making students more willing to ask direct questions that benefit performance.
