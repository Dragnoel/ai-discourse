# Samantha-Kaye Johnston

Samantha-Kaye Johnston is a researcher at the Assessment and Evaluation Research Centre, University of Melbourne, Australia. In this collection she is co-author of Vendrell and Johnston (2026), a normative pedagogical framework for LLM integration in higher education.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Vendrell and Johnston (2026) argue that the debate between banning and embracing LLMs in higher education misses the critical variable: intentional, evidence-informed scaffolding. Their framework identifies six essential epistemic processes underpinning critical engagement and translates these into eight design principles, including preserving cognitive friction and sequencing AI-mediated with AI-free phases.
