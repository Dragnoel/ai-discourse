# Andrea Valente

Andrea Valente is a researcher at York University, Canada. In this collection he is co-editor (with Banafsheh Karamifar) of Karamifar and Valente (2026), an edited volume in the *Postdisciplinary Studies in Discourse* series collecting cross-cultural discourse analyses of AI technology across media narratives.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Karamifar and Valente (2026) situate their volume within DiscourseNet's postdisciplinary programme, which emphasises exchange between discourse theory and analysis at the intersection of language and society. The collection is the first in this series to focus specifically on AI as a discursive object across cultural contexts, and functions as a venue for comparative cross-cultural AI discourse research.
