# Nele Groß

Nele Groß is a researcher at Leuphana University of Lüneburg, Germany. In this collection she is co-author of Mah and Groß (2024), a study of faculty AI self-efficacy profiles and professional development needs in higher education.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Mah and Groß (2024) identify faculty AI literacy gaps as among the most significant perceived challenges to AI integration, and find that greater equity in education is the most commonly cited perceived benefit. These findings foreground the gap between positive faculty attitudes toward AI in principle and limited readiness to integrate it in practice.
