# Isabelle Wentworth

Isabelle Wentworth is a co-author of Nikolic et al. (2024); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The systematic literature review examined teaching academics' attitudes, intentions, and behaviors toward AI and GenAI in higher education using the UTAUT framework.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Nikolic et al. (2024) identify academic integrity as a recurring significant concern across the global faculty literature, alongside reliability of AI outputs and the need for comprehensive institutional training — findings that provide an evidence base for developing faculty support frameworks.
