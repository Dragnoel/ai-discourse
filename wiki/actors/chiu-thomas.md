# Thomas Chiu

Thomas Chiu (full name Thomas K. F. Chiu) is a researcher whose institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. In this collection he is a co-author of Xia et al. (2025), the GAICS-TR scale development and validation study, where he appears to have contributed to the theoretical framework.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Xia et al. (2025) draw on a four-domain framework covering basic GenAI understanding, teaching with GenAI, research with GenAI, and professional engagement with GenAI — confirming via CFA that the scale's factor structure is valid across a diverse sample of 787 academics from nine universities, with no significant gender or age differences.
