# Tobias Wasner

Tobias Wasner is a co-author of Bassner et al. (2026); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study examines performance, learning, and cognitive load outcomes across three AI support conditions in an introductory programming course at TU Munich.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Bassner et al. (2026) use validated scales to measure intrinsic, germane, and extraneous cognitive load, finding that AI conditions reduced frustration and extraneous load without converting these reductions into learning gains — a dissociation that has implications for how cognitive load measures are interpreted in AI-enhanced learning contexts.
