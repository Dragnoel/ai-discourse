# Zachari Swiecki

Zachari Swiecki is a co-author of Cheng et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. He is associated with quantitative ethnographic and epistemic network analysis methods as applied in the study.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Cheng et al. (2025) use quantitative ethnographic methods — an approach developed within the QE/ENA research community — to code and compare help-seeking episodes across GenAI and human-tutor conditions. The methodology enables systematic comparison of interaction quality rather than only output quality.
