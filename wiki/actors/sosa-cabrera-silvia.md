# Silvia Sosa-Cabrera

Silvia Sosa-Cabrera is a researcher at a Spanish public university (specific institution not stated in this collection's source material). In this collection she is a co-author of Verano-Tacoronte et al. (2025), a PLS-SEM study of how faculty technology-related anxiety shapes ChatGPT adoption intention.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Verano-Tacoronte, Bolívar-Cruz, and Sosa-Cabrera (2025) find that anxiety about the future of the academic profession — perhaps the most visible form of AI anxiety in popular discourse — does not show a statistically significant relationship with behavioral adoption intent. This null result challenges assumptions about professional displacement anxiety as the primary adoption barrier among teaching faculty.
