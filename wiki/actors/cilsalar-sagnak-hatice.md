# Hatice Cilsalar-Sagnak

Hatice Cilsalar-Sagnak is a co-author of Xia et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study develops and validates the GAICS-TR scale for measuring higher education academics' GenAI competency self-efficacy.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Xia et al. (2025) include professional engagement with GenAI — covering ethical practice, continuing professional development, and cross-institutional collaboration — as one of the four domains in the GAICS-TR framework, reflecting an understanding of AI competency that extends beyond technical use to professional and ethical dimensions.
