# Yiming Yang

Yiming Yang is a co-author of Xia et al. (2025); institutional affiliation is not stated in this collection's source material. The study developed and validated the GAICS-TR scale for measuring GenAI competency self-efficacy in teachers and researchers in higher education.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Xia et al. (2025) validate a nine-dimension competency framework for higher education academics' GenAI self-efficacy, showing that personal factors such as gender and age showed no significant differences across scale components — supporting the instrument's generalisability across demographic groups.
