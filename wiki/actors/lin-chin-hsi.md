# Chin-Hsi Lin

Chin-Hsi Lin is a researcher at the University of Hong Kong. In this collection he is a co-author of Jin et al. (2025), a metaphor analysis study of international postgraduate students' perceptions of GenAI in academic writing at UK universities.

## Key contributions to the discourse

Jin et al. (2025) contribute a cross-cultural, cross-disciplinary empirical basis for AI literacy education, demonstrating that disciplinary background shapes how students metaphorically conceptualise GenAI and that threat perceptions (dependency, loss of voice, ethical risk) coexist alongside transformative perceptions even among active GenAI users.
