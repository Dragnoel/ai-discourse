- [ ] [[Psychological Contract]] (constructs) — Shekhar & Saurombe (2026); implicit employer-employee obligations; how algorithmic management breaches unwritten workplace agreements; distinct from formal employment contract
- [ ] [[AI Receptivity]] (constructs) — Tully et al. (2024); consumer proclivity toward AI usage; distinct from AI literacy and adoption intent; see also [[AI Literacy]] for the contradictory findings note in _review.md
