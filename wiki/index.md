# AI Discourse Wiki

This wiki maps contemporary discourse about artificial intelligence — covering
hype and capabilities claims, mysterianism, environmental cost, EdTech, labour
impact, safety, regulation, and sociocultural effects. It documents concepts,
claims, actors, incidents, and themes with sources. It does not argue a position.

## Entity types
- [[concepts/]] — terms and frames in the discourse
- [[claims/]] — specific argued positions attributed to named actors
- [[actors/]] — persons, labs, journals, advocacy organisations, vendors
- [[incidents/]] — discrete events: releases, statements, lawsuits, retractions
- [[themes/]] — recurring framings across the discourse
- [[comparisons/]] — side-by-side analysis of claims or actors
- [[constructs/]] — psychological and theoretical constructs (provisional)
- [[source/]] — one page per raw/ source file
