# Perceptions of AI

This cluster documents how different groups — faculty, students, the public, and workers — conceptualise, frame, and evaluate artificial intelligence. It covers empirical studies of attitudes and expectations, discourse analyses of how AI is constructed in institutional and popular contexts, metaphor studies, and cross-cultural comparisons of AI-related beliefs.
