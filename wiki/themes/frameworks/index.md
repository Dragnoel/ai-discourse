# Theoretical Frameworks in AI Discourse

This cluster covers papers that construct, apply, critique, or attempt to unpack theoretical and analytical frameworks used to study AI phenomena. It includes frameworks from educational technology (UTAUT, TAM, TPB), social science (SCOT, SDT, Identity-Based Motivation), cognitive psychology (Cognitive Load Theory, Conservation of Resources), and AI-specific frameworks (Human-Centered XAI, AI competency frameworks). A recurring concern in this discourse is whether frameworks developed before the generative AI era remain adequate for AI's distinctive properties.
